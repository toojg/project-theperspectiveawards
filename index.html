JULY'S AWARD THEME: ADVENTURE

Adventure takes form in many different ways and includes an array of emotions.

This July we invite you to celebrate your perspective with us by submitting your work that captures the essence of adventure and depicts it as you know it.

CELEBRATE YOUR WORK

10 finalists and 3 winners will be selected by the jury and judge for the Adventure Award Competition. Judges are looking for original and creative photos that capture and depict adventure, while using solid composition and exposure. 

We are committed to celebrating unique perspectives and recognizing photographers like you.

Join The Perspective Awards community and submit your work to this month’s Adventure Award competition.
